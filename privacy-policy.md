# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Who We Are

SIBO is a property management application operated by **Sweett** (previously Sweet Inn). SIBO is a closed platform available exclusively to authorized users and SaaS customers of Sweett.

## Data We Collect

When you use the SIBO app, we may collect the following:

- **Account information** — your name and email address used to authenticate your account
- **Usage data** — actions and interactions within the app (e.g. check-ins, task updates, messaging)
- **Device and session data** — device type, operating system, and session identifiers for security and stability purposes
- **Location data** — approximate or precise location, used to support property-related features such as on-site check-in, task management, and field operations

## How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide, operate, and improve the SIBO service — including property management, guest journeys, task management, and in-app communications. We do not use your data for advertising.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to operate the service (e.g. infrastructure providers under confidentiality obligations).

## Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. Upon account termination, data is deleted or anonymized in accordance with our internal retention policy.

## Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of material changes through the app or by email.
