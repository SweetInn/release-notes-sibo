Release Notes - November 2025 (Version P1.25)

Dear Sweet Team,

Here is what's new in This Version.
P1.25 focuses on guest transparency and booking confidence by showing cancellation policies earlier in the browsing flow, allowing guests to compare and choose before entering the booking process. This update aims to improve conversion rates by giving guests the clarity they need to confirm bookings with confidence.

Early Display of Cancellation Policies

Earn guests' confidence and improve conversion rates by showing cancellation policies earlier.

Website & App

Cancellation Policy Exposure

  • Building / Hotel Page expandable unit card shows both Flexible (Moderate) and Lite (Non-refundable) options with prices and accurate policy notes.
  • Property / Unit Page options appear in the Rates section; selecting a policy updates the price context with smooth expand/collapse animations to show the price breakdown

What's Coming Next

  • Deliveries ManagementSIBO A new foundation for managing add-on delivery services (pricing, availability, and cancellation rules) so agents can create and manage guest deliveries without manual city-by-city checks.
  • Task Scheduling FlexibilitySIBO Wider time-window support for tasks created for deliveries to make coordination easier across multiple dates.
  • Reports Smart AutomationSIBO Reports will automatically re-open when pending tasks miss their due date, with new visual indicators to simplify tracking.
  • Guidebooks CollaborationSIBO Tools for agents to request and resolve missing information in guidebooks, ensuring every property has complete, verified guest content.
