Release Notes - February 2026 (Version 27.5)

Dear Sweett Team,

In this release:

  • New feature of handling last-minute early check-in & late check-out
  • New service of Custom Breakfast Orders with pay-link
  • Improved SIBO Multi-Calendar
  • Channel manager support for Maximum length of stay
  • Consistency improvements in Lobbies Opening Hours across SIBO, Website/App, and guest journeys
Last-minute early check-in & late check-out
Clear, instant answers for last-minute requests of early check-in & late check-out.
Custom Breakfast Orders
Create custom breakfast orders with dynamic quantity per stay date, dynamic price per guest, and pay-link support.
Quick access to booking actions
All actions for a booking (Digital Keys, Early check-in, late check-out, and more) are now in one menu at the top of the page - no scrolling to the bottom to find what you need.

SIBO

Guest Experience

  • Quick access to booking actions — All actions for a booking (such as early check-in or late check-out) now live in one menu at the top of the page, so you can choose what you need without scrolling to the bottom.
  • Handle Last Minute ECI and LCO on Booking Actions provides accurate, immediate responses for last-minute early check-in and late check-out requests made from Booking Details, Actions menu.

Direct Sales & Services

  • Support Custom Breakfast Orders lets agents create custom breakfast orders per stay date with dynamic pricing and pay-link support.

Consistency and Reliability

  • Lobby Opening Hours Consistency standardizes and correctly displays hotel lobby opening hours across SIBO and the website.

Integrations

  • Send Maximum Length of Stay to Rentals United syncs the maximum length of stay restriction to Rentals United to ensure booking limits are enforced (currently applied to hotels).
  • Rentals United Base-Price Logic Deprecation removes deprecated Rentals United base-price logic and relies exclusively on explicit nightly pricing to improve channel compatibility and pricing accuracy.
  • Fix Booking.com Cancellation Policy Recognition ensures Booking.com reservations are assigned the correct cancellation policy for all partners.
  • Fix Rentals United Alterations Reverting After Update resolves a timing conflict between API updates and incoming webhooks that could revert booking alterations.

Website & App

Online Check-In and Guest Dashboard

  • Handle Last Minute ECI and LCO requests on Online Check-In and Guest Dashboard provides accurate, immediate responses for last-minute early check-in and late check-out requests.
  • WhatsApp Default Contact Method sets WhatsApp as the default contact method in Online Check-In for non-Airbnb guests.

Consistency and Reliability

  • Lobby Opening Hours Consistency standardizes and correctly displays hotel lobby opening hours across the website and SIBO.

Enhancements & Bug Fixes

  • Enhanced Multi-Calendar View for Single Property UnitsSIBO flattens buildings with single-property units to reduce UI clutter and make availability easier to scan.
  • Multi-Calendar Property Name VisibilitySIBO adds a Specs toggle to hide property attribute icons and free up space for displaying full property names.
  • Smoother Multi-Calendar ExperienceSIBO delays tooltips slightly on hover, so they do not flash while moving across multiple bookings or blocked dates.
  • Bug Fix Website Services Date ConsistencyWebsite/App fixes inconsistent service dates shown to guests in different time zones and locations.
  • Bug Fix Avoid Page Crash on Trailing Slash LinksWebsite/App ensures shared or bookmarked links load correctly even when the URL includes a trailing slash, by applying sensible defaults instead of showing errors.
  • Bug Fix Fix Unit Page Not Opening on Adama WebsiteWebsite/App resolves an issue where unit pages on the Adama partner website could fail to load and show an error instead of unit details.
  • Bug Fix Fix User Mentions Not Sharing ConversationsSIBO ensures that mentioning a user in a comment automatically shares the conversation with them.
  • Bug Fix Invoice Breakfast Included Value FixSIBO resolves an issue where invoices incorrectly showed Breakfast included = 0 when breakfast was paid as part of the stay.
