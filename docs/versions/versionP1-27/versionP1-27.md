# Version P1.27

**Released:** Feb 05, 2026

P1.27 focuses on improving booking clarity and conversion, strengthening guest continuity across sessions, and expanding payment flexibility across platforms. It also includes the new Adama partner website, shifted from the old white label website to the new one.

### What's New?

#### <u>Website & App</u>

**Apple Pay — Expanded Payment Flexibility:**
- **Windows users** — Apple Pay available as a pay-link guests can complete from their mobile.
- **Mac Chrome users** — native Apple cross-device experience.
- **iPhone App users** — full native Apple Pay flow.
- Enables faster checkout and reduces payment friction.

**Booking Flow Step 2 Redesign:**
- Makes the booking process clearer by presenting policies, options, and totals in a more transparent way.
- Helps guests confirm with confidence and with fewer surprises before payment.

**Recent Search Results:**
- Keeps the latest search context (city, hotel, apartment, dates, guests) so guests can continue where they left off.

**Booking Dashboard Link in Online Check-In:**
- Adds a direct link to the Stay Dashboard when guests finish the Online Check-In.
- Gives guests quick access to manage their stay and access digital keys.

#### <u>Partners</u>

- Released the new **Adama Partner Website** experience.

{: .note }
The Adama partner site will be fully live in the coming days as rollout completes.

#### <u>Enhancements & Bug Fixes</u>

- Fixed — low contrast in the header logo on dark-themed headers.
- Fixed — Online Check-In links that were already expired were being sent to early bookers.
- Fixed — incorrect totals when both a promotion and a coupon are applied, keeping prices consistent from review to payment.
- Fixed — crashes that could occur when guests used browser translation on the website.
- Fixed — breakfast is no longer shown in guest bookings when the hotel does not offer breakfast.
- Enhancement — updated wording across several pages to use consistent Room and Apartment terminology.
