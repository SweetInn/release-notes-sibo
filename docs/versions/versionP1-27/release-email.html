Release Notes - January 2026 (Version P1.27)

Dear Sweet Team,

Release Jan 2026 (Version P1.27), focuses on improving booking clarity and conversion, strengthening guest continuity across sessions, and expanding payment flexibility across platforms.
It also includes a new Adama partner website (Shifted from the old White label website to the new one).

Website & App

Expanding payment flexibility across platforms

  • Added Apple Pay as a payment option for:
    - Windows users as a pay-link they can complete from their mobile;
    - Mac Chrome users as a native Apple cross device experience;
    - iPhone App users as full Apple pay native flow;
    enabling faster checkout and reducing payment friction.

Improving booking clarity and conversion

  • Booking Flow Step 2 Redesign makes the booking process clearer by presenting policies, options, and totals in a more transparent way, helping guests confirm with confidence.

Guest continuity & conversion

  • Recent Search Results keeps the latest search context (city, hotel, apartment, dates, guests) so guests can continue where left of.

Guest clarity

  • Booking Dashboard Link in Online Check-In adds a direct link to the Stay Dashboard when guest finish the online check-in, giving guests quick access to manage their stay and access the digital keys.


Partners

Partners

  • Released the new Adama Partner Website experience. (It will be fully live in the coming days as rollout completes.)
Apple Pay Support

Guests can now pay with Apple Pay on both the App and Website for a faster checkout experience.

Recent Search Results

Guests can return to their most recent search results and continue browsing without re-entering details.

Booking Flow Step 2 Redesign

A clearer booking step that helps guests review policies, options, and pricing before payment, with fewer surprises.

Enhancements & Bug Fixes

  • Bug Fix Header Logo Contrast Website/App Fixed low contrast in the header logo on dark-themed headers.
  • Bug Fix Expired Online Check-In Link for Early Bookers Website/App Prevented sending Online Check-In links that were already expired.
  • Bug Fix Pricing Accuracy with Promotions and Coupons Website/App Prevents incorrect totals when both a promotion and a coupon are applied, keeping prices consistent from review to payment.
  • Enhancement Terminology Alignment Website/App Updated wording across several pages to use consistent Room and Apartment terminology.
  • Bug Fix Google Translate Page Stability Website/App Fixed crashes that could occur when guests used browser translation on the website.
  • Bug Fix Breakfast Service Visibility Website/App Breakfast is no longer shown in guest bookings when the hotel does not offer breakfast.
