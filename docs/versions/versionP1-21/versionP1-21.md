# Version P1.21

**Released:** Aug 19, 2025

This version is all about **Inventory Management** — with key supporting updates in No-Show handling and the first steps of the new SIBO interface.

### What's New?

#### <u>Inventory Management</u>

![Inventory Management - Global View]({{ "/assets/images/versionP1.21/1211.png" | relative_url }})

![Inventory Management - Settings]({{ "/assets/images/versionP1.21/1212.png" | relative_url }})

Gain full control over your stock. Track items globally and per property, use templates tailored to property typologies, and rely on a complete audit trail for confident decisions.

- **Settings Management** — configure storage locations, products, categories, brands, and unit types.
- **Invoice Management** — fill stock with purchase invoices and track expenses.
- **Global Inventory Tracking** — centralized view with advanced filters (model, size, color, status, and more).
- **Property-Level Inventory** — manage stock at each individual property.
- **Unit Types Management** — with detailed descriptions.
- **Inventory Templates & Typology Assignment** — linked to property types by bedroom count or capacity.
- **Audit Trail** — full visibility on all changes.

#### <u>Enhancements & Bug Fixes</u>

- **No-Show Handling** — mark a booking as No-Show with confirmation safeguards, which instantly releases the calendar dates for resale, automatically cancels related tasks, and revokes digital key access.
- **SIBO — Refreshed Interface** — modern login options (Google/Microsoft), smooth cross-navigation back to old SIBO, Light/Dark themes, language support, and a responsive design.
- **Infrastructure** — upgraded to the latest stable Node.js version, enabling improved performance and unlocking AI capabilities.
- Fixed — "Invalid date" errors on the pricing page when updating rates.
- Fixed — sticky header behavior across the interface.
- Fixed — abandoned booking status update logic.
- Fixed — a website crash related to seasonal opening hours.
- Fixed — improved Booking.com scores parser handling.

#### <u>What's Coming Next</u>

- **Partner White Label Site** *(Website)* — launching the first partner site with integrated SIBO quoting system.
- **Login by Email Link** *(Website/App)* — new option for guests to log in via a secure link sent directly to their email.
- **Login Flow Redesign** *(Website/App)* — complete redesign of the registration and login experience.
- **Booking Flow Update** *(Website/App)* — incorporating the new login method and reorganizing booking steps for a smoother flow.
- **UI/UX Enhancements** *(Website/App)* — updated form fields and a unified color palette across the website.
- **Inventory Management Enhancements** *(SIBO)* — advanced features including inventory-inspection-based tasks, auto-report missing items lists, task completion tied to inventory availability, item transfers between storage locations, and more.
- **Accommodation Type Filters** *(SIBO)* — new filters by accommodation type in Bookings, Dashboard, and Cleanings.
- **Custom User Filters** *(SIBO)* — save and reuse your most common booking searches.
