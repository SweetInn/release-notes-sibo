# Version P1.22

**Released:** Oct 15, 2025

P1.22 focuses on full lifecycle management for reports and tasks, structured support for common areas, smarter booking filters, partner white label foundations, and a streamlined connect flow on the website and app.

### What's New?

#### <u>SIBO</u>

**Reports and Tasks:**
- **Chained Tasks and Reports System** — manage an issue lifecycle completely with chained tasks, richer resolutions, re-opening when not fixed, and inventory usage attached to tasks. Gives full visibility and control from first report to final closure with accountable outcomes.
- **Nuki Smart Locks Automation** — low battery alerts on locks and keypads automatically create reports and maintenance tasks with provider-level controls. Ensures timely replacements and fewer lockouts without manual triage.
- **Enhanced Task Display** — task cards now show booking info, guest preparation details, and tags on both mobile and desktop. Improves team context so work starts faster and errors are reduced.

**Common Areas Management:**
- **Shared Spaces in Operations** — add and track building common areas in tasks, cleanings, and reports with filters for building and area. Organizes work across stairwells, rooftops, and lobbies so scheduling and reporting stay accurate.

**Smarter Booking Filters:**
- **Presets and Custom Filters** — save frequently used booking filter combinations and manage your own custom filters. Shortens search time and keeps teams in flow.
- **Accommodation Type Filter** — unified accommodation type filter across key screens. Improves property categorization and speeds up discovery.

#### <u>Website & App</u>

- **New Connect Flow and Authentication** — passwordless login and registration via secure email link. Simplifies access and reduces friction for new and returning users.
- **Enhanced Booking Flow** — guest details are handled more cleanly for both registered and new users to improve conversion and reduce abandonment.
- **Partners Website & Quotes** — preparation of the new website template and quote system for SaaS partners.

#### <u>Enhancements & Bug Fixes</u>

- **Removal of Postpone** — the Postpone action was removed and replaced with clear rescheduling workflows and confirmations for in-stay bookings. Eliminates confusion so task state and timing are always correct.
- Fixed — visibility toggle (eye icon) in the new connect flow password field.
- Fixed — AI Chatbot accuracy: improved response handling and relevance for more helpful answers and fewer escalations.
- Fixed — some scheduled credit card payments were repeatedly failing.
- Fixed — clicking a building's unit link was resetting the selected language to English.
- Fixed — missing translations in the Online Check-In flow.

#### <u>What's Coming Next</u>

- **Knowledge Base** *(SIBO)* — central hub to manage and find company SOPs, accessible for SIBO users and the AI chatbot.
- **Guidebooks** *(SIBO)* — new tab in booking details with structured property and city guidebooks for use by SIBO users and the AI chatbot.
- **Reports — Fix Access Preferences** *(SIBO)* — clearer info collection so operations know exactly when to schedule fixes.
- **Common Area Filters** *(SIBO)* — new filters by building and shared spaces in the reports screen.
- **Reports Flow Enhancement** *(SIBO)* — assistive prompt to prevent reports from remaining stuck as Re-Opened during workflow.
- **Reports Team Dimension** *(SIBO)* — ability to filter and group reports by the responsible team based on category.
- **Nuki Daily Device Wake-Up** *(SIBO)* — proactive sync to keep smart lock statuses and battery alerts accurate every day.
- **Hotel OCI Enhancements** *(SIBO)* — smoother hotel check-ins with direct webcam capture and agent ability to confirm house rules on behalf of guests.
